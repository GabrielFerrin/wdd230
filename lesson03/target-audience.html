TARGET AUDIENCE

The page is targeted towards business owners, companies looking for providers and strategic partners, entrepreneurs, residents in general of the area of Manta who want information about the commerce situation.

PERSONA

A business owner who has a large and successful company in the area of Manta.

PERSONA

An entrepreneur who wants to start a business in the area of Manta.

SCENARIO 1

The business manager has an issue with a new regulation that was recently implemented related to certain taxes. They want to have information about what to do to be aligned with this new regulation to avoid penalties and fees.

SCENARIO 2

Persona 1 wants to propose a fair event that can help publicize information about the businesses of the area and needs the contact information to do so.

SCENARIO 3

Persona 2 wants to have a sense of the kind of businesses around the area in order to find providers as well as customer and strategic partners.

SCENARIO 4

Persona 2 wants to find out the legal requirements to implement their company

SCENARIO 5

Persona 1 wants to submit information about an important achievement in which the Chamber of Commerce page was part of their success to be shared as a testimonial for the page.
