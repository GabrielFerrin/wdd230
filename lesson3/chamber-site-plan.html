Chamber of Comerce - Site Plan

SITE NAME

Manta Chamber of Commerce | This name represents the city in which the chamber operates.

SITE PURPOSE

    Alignment | Manta Chamber of Commerce web enables businesses in the region and the public in general to be updated with the endeavors of the Chamber of Commerce, which are: To help businesses raise their profiles, expand their networks, develop their knowledge and understanding of policy and opportunities they face, and be aligned with regulatory alignments. This page will also help businesses and the public in general to be informed about the events program which can be around 300 a year.

SCENARIOS

1 | A business manager has an issue with a new regulation that was recently implemented related to certain taxes. They want to have information about what to do to be aligned with this new regulation to avoid penalties and fees.

2 | A business owner wants to propose a fair event that can help publicize information about the businesses of the area and needs the contact information to do so.

3 | An entrepreneur wants to have an idea of the kind of businesses around the area in order to find providers as well as customer and strategic partners.
